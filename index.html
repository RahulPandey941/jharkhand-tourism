Our Mission

Tourists often struggle to access reliable information about destinations, transportation, accommodations, and cultural activities. Meanwhile, local artisans, tribal communities, and small-scale service providers remain excluded from the digital ecosystem. Our platform bridges this gap — offering authentic information while connecting and empowering local communities.

Join us in making tourism in Jharkhand inclusive & sustainable.
